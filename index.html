Janarkush: The Song of the Mountains

An animated legend of harmony, courage, and the song that saved a world.

Inspired by Kyrgyz mythology.

Synopsis

In a magical mountain world where humans once spoke the language of birds, twin brothers must protect their family and homeland from the return of Ka’ar — an ancient sorcerer.

Guided by their mother Aysal and the mythical bird Janarkush, they uncover their legacy and craft a sacred instrument to defeat darkness.

A poetic animated legend about harmony, music, and courage without violence.
