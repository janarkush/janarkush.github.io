Janarkush: The Song of the Mountains

An animated legend of harmony, courage, and the song that saved a world.

Inspired by Kyrgyz mythology.

Synopsis

When an ancient evil sorcerer rises to silence the mythical bird Janarkush and plunge the world into imbalance, twin brothers must overcome their fears and differences to protect their family and the natural harmony.

They face betrayal, curses, and magical forces — and in the end, transform a cursed creation into a sacred musical instrument that brings healing instead of destruction.

A poetic animated tale inspired by Kyrgyz mythology — about music instead of violence, courage without cruelty, and harmony as resistance.
