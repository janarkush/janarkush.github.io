Janarkush: The Song of the Mountains

A poetic animated legend of harmony, courage, and the song that saved a world.

Inspired by Kyrgyz mythology.

Logline

When an ancient evil rises, twin brothers must protect a mythical bird and transform a cursed creation into a timeless instrument of harmony.

Synopsis

In a magical world where humans once spoke the language of birds, a dark sorcerer returns to silence the harmony of nature. Twin brothers must face fear, betrayal, and curses to protect their family and awaken balance. Their journey ends not with war — but with music.

Main Characters

  • Kambar – Father, protector, moral compass.
  • Aysal – Silent strength and maternal wisdom.
  • Aksuu & Koksuu – Twin boys, opposite energies, same courage.
  • Ka’ar – Sorcerer of destruction.
  • Kashan – Witch of laziness and false sparkle.
  • Janarkush – Mythical bird of balance, inspired by Umai.

Visual World

The visual style blends poetic animation with cultural realism. Inspired by mountain landscapes, nomadic patterns, and ancient symbology — rooted in the Kyrgyz spirit.
